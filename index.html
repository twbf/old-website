About Myself

This is my website devoted to myself, Thomas Bueler. My family and I are living in Boulder, Colorado for the year, but we usually live in Fairbanks, Alaska. We are living in Boulder because my parents are Mathematicians, and they have sabbatical. A sabbatical is when a professor can go anywhere in the world to do research with other mathematicians. It has been great living in Boulder for a year. The weather here is sunny and warm compared to Alaska although it still snows here. I created this website so I could learn HTML and CSS. I hope you approve of my design. The latest edit on this website was on 07-04-2015. My email address is twbf.public@gmail.com
